How it works

Six disciplines, one private agent. Daniel personally drives every step — from the first conversation to a signed offer.

01 · Onboarding•02 · Job Applications•03 · Outreach•04 · Interviews•05 · Negotiations•06 · Success•
  1. 01

    Begins Day 1

    Onboarding

    We define your goals, target roles, and search strategy. Resume, LinkedIn, and outreach templates are fully optimized so the search launches with clarity and confidence.

    • Guided onboarding with a clear roadmap
    • Resume and LinkedIn rewrite
    • Target company and role list defined together
  2. 02

    Week 1 onward

    Job Applications

    Real humans on our team apply to 50–100 vetted, on-target roles per week. Each application is reviewed and approved by you inside a shared dashboard.

    • Customized resume per posting
    • Applications inside 1 hour of posting where possible
    • Screen-recorded for QA
  3. 03

    Alongside every application

    Outreach

    For every role we apply to, we reach out to the recruiter and hiring manager directly — turning a cold listing into a warm conversation.

    • Personalized recruiter messages
    • Hiring-manager outreach on LinkedIn
    • Follow-ups scheduled and tracked
  4. 04

    As invitations land

    Interviews

    We prep you for every loop — behavioral, technical, executive panel — with tactical strategy and live mocks so you walk in calm and persuasive.

    • Mock interviews with feedback
    • Loop strategy and stakeholder mapping
    • Story bank you can reuse
  5. 05

    Once offers arrive

    Negotiations

    We help you structure the counter, protect your leverage, and walk into the close with a clear number — on base, bonus, equity, and start date.

    • Market data on comp benchmarks
    • Counter scripts written with you
    • Multi-offer leverage strategy
  6. 06

    Sign and close

    Success

    You accept. We close out the file. The search ends — and you start the next chapter with the right role in hand.

    • Offer signed
    • Start-date planning
    • Optional 30-day check-in
